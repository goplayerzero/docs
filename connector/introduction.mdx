PlayerZero connects to your existing stack through integrations. These OAuth-based connections require minimal setup and enable AI agents to access context from your tools.

## Supported Integrations

### Project Management
- [Linear](/connector/pm/linear) - Issue tracking for software teams
- [Jira](/connector/pm/jira) - Agile project management
- [Azure DevOps](/connector/pm/azure-devops) - Development collaboration
- [Confluence](/connector/pm/confluence) - Team documentation

### CRM
- [Salesforce](/connector/crm/salesforce) - Customer relationship management
- [HubSpot](/connector/crm/hubspot) - Marketing and sales platform

### Support
- [Zendesk](/connector/ticketing/zendesk) - Customer service platform
- [ServiceNow](/connector/ticketing/servicenow) - IT service management

## What Integrations Enable

Once connected, PlayerZero AI agents can:
- Access tickets, issues, and documentation from your tools
- Take actions like creating comments or updating statuses
- Provide context-aware assistance using data from your stack
