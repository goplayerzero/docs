[Salesflare](https://salesflare.com/) is an intelligent CRM for small B2B businesses and startups, which fills out your address book and keeps track of all interactions.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Salesflare”.
5. Login to your Salesflare account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Salesflare project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Salesflare connector.
