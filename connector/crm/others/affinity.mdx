[Affinity](https://www.affinity.co/) is a relationship intelligence platform designed to help teams manage and grow their networks.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Affinity”.
5. Login to your Affinity account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Affinity project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Affinity connector.
