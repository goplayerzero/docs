[Close](https://www.close.com/) is a sales engagement CRM designed to help SMBs turn more leads into revenue.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Close”.
5. Login to your Close account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Close project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Close connector.
