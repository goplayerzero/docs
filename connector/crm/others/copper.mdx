[Copper](https://www.copper.com/) is a CRM software designed for and recommended by Google, made for G Suite users.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Copper”.
5. Login to your Copper account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Copper project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Copper connector.
