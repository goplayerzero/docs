[Dynamics 365 Sales](https://dynamics.microsoft.com/en-us/sales/overview/) is Microsoft’s CRM software that enables greater sales productivity and marketing effectiveness.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Dynamics 365 Sales”.
5. Login to your Dynamics 365 Sales account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Dynamics 365 Sales project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Dynamics 365 Sales connector.
