[Keap](https://keap.com/) provides sales and marketing automation software for small businesses, formerly known as Infusionsoft.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Keap”.
5. Login to your Keap account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Keap project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Keap connector.
