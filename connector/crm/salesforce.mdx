[Salesforce](https://www.salesforce.com/) is an American cloud-based software company that provides CRM service and enterprise applications.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Salesforce”.
5. Login to your Salesforce account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Salesforce project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Salesforce connector.
