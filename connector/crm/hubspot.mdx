[Hubspot](https://www.hubspot.com/) is an industry leader in marketing, sales, and customer service software products.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Hubspot”.
5. Login to your Hubspot account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Hubspot project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Hubspot connector.
