[Zoho CRM](https://www.zoho.com/crm/) is an online CRM software for managing your sales, marketing, and support in a single system.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Zoho CRM”.
5. Login to your Zoho CRM account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Zoho CRM project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Zoho CRM connector.
