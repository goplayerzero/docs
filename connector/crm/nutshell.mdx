[Nutshell](https://www.nutshell.com/) is a sales automation CRM and email marketing tool for small businesses.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Nutshell”.
5. Login to your Nutshell account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Nutshell project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Nutshell connector.
