[SugarCRM](https://www.sugarcrm.com/) is a customer relationship management (CRM) solutions provider.

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”CRM” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “SugarCRM”.
5. Login to your SugarCRM account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your SugarCRM project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the SugarCRM connector.
