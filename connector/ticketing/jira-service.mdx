Front is a customer communication platform that combines emails, apps, and teammates into a single view.

<Snippet file="ticketing-connector.mdx" />

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Select the Front connector under the Tickets section.
3. Login to your Front account if not already logged in.
4. Enter your Front project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
5. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Front connector.
