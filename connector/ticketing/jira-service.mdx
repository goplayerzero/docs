## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Select the Jira Service connector under the Tickets section.
3. Login to your Jira Service account if not already logged in.
4. Enter your Jira Service project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
5. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Jira Service connector.
