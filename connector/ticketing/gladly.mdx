[Gladly](https://www.gladly.com/) is a customer service platform that puts people at the center of a single, lifelong conversation.

<Snippet file="ticketing-connector.mdx" />

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Select the Gladly connector under the Tickets section.
3. Login to your Gladly account if not already logged in.
4. Enter your Gladly project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
5. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Gladly connector.
