[Kustomer](https://www.kustomer.com/) is a customer service CRM platform focused on understanding and serving modern customers.

<Snippet file="ticketing-connector.mdx" />

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Select the Kustomer connector under the Tickets section.
3. Login to your Kustomer account if not already logged in.
4. Enter your Kustomer project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
5. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Kustomer connector.
