[Spotdraft](https://www.spotdraft.com/) is an AI-driven platform for automating legal contract management and analysis.

<Snippet file="pm-connector.mdx" />

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”Ticketing” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Spotdraft”.
5. Login to your Spotdraft account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Spotdraft project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Spotdraft connector.
