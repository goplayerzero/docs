## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Select the Zoho Desk connector under the Tickets section.
4. Login to your Zoho Desk account if not already logged in.
5. Enter your Zoho Desk project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
6. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Zoho Desk connector.
