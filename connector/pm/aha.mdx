[Aha!](https://www.aha.io/) is a roadmap software tool that helps teams define their strategy and manage projects.

<Snippet file="pm-connector.mdx" />

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”Project Management” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Aha!”.
5. Login to your Aha! account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Aha! project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Aha! connector.
