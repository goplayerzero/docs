[Adobe Workfront](https://business.adobe.com/products/workfront/main.html) is an online project management solution that manages work, projects, and tasks.

<Snippet file="pm-connector.mdx" />

## Setup
1. From the PlayerZero web app, navigate to the home page.
2. Click on the “Connectors” button.
3. Filter by the ”Project Management” group in the dropdown.
4. Search for “Adobe Workfront”.
5. Login to your Adobe Workfront account if not already logged in.
6. Enter your Adobe Workfront project domain or the appropriate API key for your project.
7. Authorize Merge to read and write data through the Adobe Workfront connector.
