description: "Use PlayerZero to analyze dependencies, recommend migration paths, and preserve functionality during modernization."
---

Framework or language migrations are risky and complex. PlayerZero helps teams **analyze dependencies, identify risks, and plan migrations** so functionality is preserved.

---

## Example Prompts to Try

- “List all dependencies affected by our `.NET → .NET Core` migration.”  
- “Suggest a migration plan for moving the `AuthService` from AngularJS to React.”  
- “Show which modules require refactoring before we can upgrade Node.js.”  

## Additional Tips

PlayerZero simplifies migrations by:  
- **Mapping out impacted services and modules** automatically.  
- **Highlighting risk points** like tight couplings or outdated APIs.  
- **Offering migration suggestions** aligned with modern best practices.

## Adapting PlayerZero to Your Environment

PlayerZero can:  
- Provide **risk-aware migration roadmaps**.  
- Analyze **dependencies and refactor requirements**.  
- Suggest **code rewrites** aligned to your standards.
