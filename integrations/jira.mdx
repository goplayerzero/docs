### Create a Jira ticket directly from a PlayerZero issue

Rather than manually copying all high level issue information into Jira, simply
generate a ticket directly from the PlayerZero report.

![](/images/ezgif.com-gif-maker_43.gif)![](/images/ezgif.com-gif-maker_43.gif "Click to close...")

### Jira tags and statuses sync automatically to PlayerZero issues

Quickly understand the activity and progress of a specific issue without needing
to view the Jira ticket.

![](/images/Screen_Shot_2022-07-14_at_11.21_1.png)![](/images/Screen_Shot_2022-07-14_at_11.21_1.png "Click to close...")

### Setup

1. Go to Integrations settings > integrations in the PlayerZero menu

![](/images/Screen_Shot_2022-07-12_at_6.49.51_PM.png)![](/images/Screen_Shot_2022-07-12_at_6.49.51_PM.png "Click to close...")

1. Click "configure" and connect the project you want.

![](/images/Screen_Shot_2022-07-12_at_6.50.42_PM.png)![](/images/Screen_Shot_2022-07-12_at_6.50.42_PM.png "Click to close...")
